Popular questions

How can we help?

We aim to provide a bespoke service experience to help you meet the needs of your patients. Find below detailed answers about Clinigen Direct, regulation, purchasing, shipping and delivery.

Who are Clinigen? What is Clinigen Direct? What is Cliniport?

Please visit the About Clinigen page or watch this short video.

Do you import medicines? How are you different to a pharmaceutical wholesaler?

We are a global business with offices in 14 countries and our ability to source and distribute medicines all over the world is what makes us different to pharmaceutical wholesalers who only serve their domestic markets. We exist to make sure a healthcare professional with a patient in need, anywhere in the world, can always get the right medicine for their individual patient – quickly, easily and safely whether licensed or unlicensed.

Do you provide ‘Specials’?

‘Specials’ can be provided to healthcare professionals by our sister companies, Quantum Pharmaceutical (www.quantumpharmaceutical.co.uk) in the UK and QM Specials (www.qmspecials.ie) in Ireland.

In which countries do you hold pharmaceutical wholesale licenses?

We hold wholesale licenses for the supply of medicinal products in the EU (UK, Ireland), South Africa, Australia and Singapore.

Can I download or view a full product list?

In some countries it is not permissible to promote the use of individual medicines on an unlicensed basis and, as such, we do not display a single list of the medicines we can supply. Please use the search bar at the top of this page to find the medicine you are looking for or, if it does not appear immediately, click the ‘Looking for something else?’ button to create a bespoke sourcing request.

Can you get a Certificate of Authenticity or approved product information (SPC, PI) with all of your products?

We have a specialist in-house Medical Information, Quality and Regulatory Affairs teams who support our Customer Services team when specific documentation is required.

How does your customer service team work?

Our customer service team consists of over 120 people in offices around the world. Together, we speak more than 25 languages and have an unrivalled practical knowledge of how to supply and distribute unlicensed medicines. Our UK operations were awarded the ServiceMark accreditation from The Institute of Customer Service and we are now pursuing a global accreditation.

How can I find out more about your Quality Standards and Quality Management System?

Read more about our approach to Quality, both internally and across our supply chain.

Do you over-label all of your products?

We have in-house over-labelling capabilities, so can provide over-labelling when required.
