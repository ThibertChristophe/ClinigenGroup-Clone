Love Your Pharmacist Awards 2021

Celebrating excellence in hospital pharmacy.

Love Your Pharmacist Awards 2020

We unfortunately weren't able to announce the winners of the Love Your Pharmacist Awards 2020 last year, however it is our absolute pleasure to be able to do so now.

Award categories

Hospital Pharmacist of the Year Award

Celebrating a pharmacist who has consistently demonstrated a commitment to innovation, excellence and success over the past 12 months.

Lifetime Achievement Award

Acknowledging a pharmacist who has gone above and beyond over the duration of their career.

Rising Star Award

Celebrating a pharmacist who has shown real ambition and innovation in the first 5 years of their career.

The Extra Mile Award

Celebrating those who have gone the extra mile and explored all options to achieve a better patient outcome.

Pharmacist Team of the Year Award

Celebrating team spirit, collaboration and success within hospital pharmacy teams.

Entry criteria

Nominations are now closed. To be eligible for an award, nominees must be a practising hospital pharmacist operating within Europe. Candidates may be nominated for multiple awards, however, a nominator may only nominate one candidate for each award. Nominations are now closed.
